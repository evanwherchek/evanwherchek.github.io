Introduction

Howdy! My name is Evan and I am currently an undergraduate student at Texas A&M studying for my Bachelor of Arts in Computing with a Concentration Area in Software Entrepreneurship. I am on my final semester and will graduate in May of 2024. I'm interested in creating more accessible products because I believe that software is a transformative experience that anyone should be able to use no matter who they are. I've been obsessed with computers since I was very young and I believe that it's the closest thing that you can have to super powers. It would be a really sad life for someone if they were cut off from being able to experience the joy of using them. In addition to being a software engineer, I am also a student of entrepreneurship and I believe in the business value of making your technology accessible. Any decision making leader in a business should recognize that making their products accessible can only widen their customer base and lead to greater sales while also providing excellent publicity.
